Interactive In-Depth Gap Analysis

Explore opportunities for growth across all dimensions

How to use this view:

Use the dropdown menu below to select a dimension and view all of the opportunities associated with that dimension, ranked in order of community interest.

Once a dimension is selected, the chart reveals the gap between community priorities and organizational programming:

  • Green numbers show that community priority is higher than programming offered (opportunities for growth).
  • Red numbers show that program offerings are higher than community priority.
