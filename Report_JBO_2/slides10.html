Instructions:

The following slide examines internal alignment between board and staff members on organizational priorities. Understanding consensus requires a two-step approach.

Step 1: Examine the rWG Index. Look at the colored dots to identify areas of strong agreement (green), moderate agreement (yellow), or weak agreement (red). The rWG score quantifies the level of consensus within the organization.

Consensus Levels (rWG):
High (≥.70): Strong agreement
Medium (.50–.69): Moderate agreement
Low (<.50): Weak agreement

Step 2: Examine the Distribution (especially for red dots). When consensus is weak, review the percentage breakdown across Low, Medium, and High ratings to understand the diversity of opinions. Note: Exercise caution when interpreting percentages, particularly with smaller response samples.
